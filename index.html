Why I Like Baseball

Baseball is my favorite sport because it is competitive and difficult.
I have to challenge myself and my teammates to get better. I have played
since I was just four years old and my father has taught me nearly everything
I know. Now I play for the team at Licking Heights along with the NC Bruins
travel team.

Cincinnati Reds

Aside from playing baseball, I aslo enjoy watching it. My favorite team is
the Cincinnati Reds because my entire family is from Cincinnati and support
the team. I have been to many Reds games in the past and my favorite player,
a newer addition to the team is second baseman Scooter Gennett. He is an
inspiring player and I love to watch him play.
